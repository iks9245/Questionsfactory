## Target Customer Profile (validated)

**Primary**: Tech or consulting companies, 50-300 employees, already subscribed to Copilot for Microsoft 365 or ChatGPT Enterprise. Have a dedicated L&D or HR function. Their procurement contacts are: L&D Manager, CTO/VP Engineering, or HR Director.

**Why they'll pay**: They're already paying $30-100/user/month for AI tools and need to justify that spend to leadership. A $25/user/month training tool that produces ROI evidence is a easy sell.

**Why now**: Enterprise AI contract renewals are happening in 2025-2026. Companies need renewal justification data.
